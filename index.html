About the Company

Built on Purpose.
Rooted in Nigeria.

Halaska Homes Limited is a Nigerian real estate development and property management company established to redefine what luxury living means in Africa's most dynamic property market. From our first project, we have operated with a singular conviction: that quality construction, transparent dealings, and long-term stewardship produce outcomes that benefit not only our clients, but the communities and economy around them.

2026 — Founding
Incorporated in Nigeria

Halaska Homes Limited was formally registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria (RC No. 9507179), headquartered in Nigeria with a mandate to operate across the country's primary real estate markets.

2026 — Market Entry
Portfolio Launch

We launched our inaugural portfolio across Lagos and Abuja, targeting high-demand residential segments — from premium family homes in Ikoyi and Lekki to executive serviced apartments on Victoria Island and Eko Atlantic.

Near-Term
National Expansion

We are actively building a presence in Port Harcourt, Calabar, Kano, and other emerging urban centres, with plans to extend our property management services to commercial and institutional clients nationwide.

Our long-term objective is to be recognised as Nigeria's foremost luxury real estate developer — a company synonymous with structural integrity, financial transparency, and an enduring contribution to the built environment.
